About Me

I graduated from University of Waterloo with a master's degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering and a bachelor's in Nanotechnology Engineering. My research was focused on writing MATLAB code to describe the quantum physics in semiconductor materials. I am also an artist who works with the oil, pencil, and digital mediums. In my free time, I enjoy dabbling in various technologies, drinking coffee, and playing animal crossing.
